# Registration (User Guide)

## Create an account
1. Open `/register`.
2. Enter your full name, email, and password.
3. Complete the bot protection question.
4. Accept the terms of service checkbox.
5. Submit the form.

You will see a success message and receive an email with a verification link.

## Verify your email
- Open the verification link from the email.
- You should see “Email verified. You can now log in.”

## If you didn’t receive the email
- Check spam/junk folders.
- Request another verification email from the app (or contact an admin in production deployments).
